Statement of Norms, Expectations, and Corrective Actions
Ref: FA08-74-DIAGTOOL

Norms:
	 All team members will be notified 48 hours in advance to any changes in the meeting schedule.
	 No team member will be more than 15 minutes late to a meeting without prior notification.
	 All team members are to bring their design notebook to every meeting.
	 All team members are expected to contribute  the cost of any and all expenses that arise from the project.
	
Expectations:
	 Team members are expected to treat one another with respect.
	 Team members will help one another when deemed necessary.
	 Team members will ask for help when they first realize they need it and not wait until the last minute to request it.
	 Team members may offer constructive criticism, but may not bash, insult, put down, or otherwise demoralize other team members.
	 Anything that is not written in the team's design notebooks is to be published to the team repository.

Corrective Actions:
	 Verbal warning from any or all team members.
	 Verbal warning from the project manager.
	 Written warning from the project manager and a verbal report to the course professors.
	 Written report to the course professors.
